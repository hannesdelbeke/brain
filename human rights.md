### Freedom & equality
1. All human beings are born free and equal in dignity and rights
2. Everyone is entitled to all the human rights and freedoms
3. Everyone has the right to life, liberty and security of person
4. No one shall be held in slavery or servitude;
### No pain
1. No one shall be subjected to torture or to cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment or punishment - [[pain]]
### equal in Law
1. recognition everywhere as a person before the law
2. equal before the law
3. effective remedy if these human rights are violated
4. Not subjected to arbitrary arrest
5. entitled to a fair and public hearing
6. innocent until proved guilty
