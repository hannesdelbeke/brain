## Don't
People in the [[note taking]] community often get obsessed with [[writing notes about notes|writing notes about note taking]].  
It's exciting when you discover a "better" way of thinking, wanting to share it with everyone. 

### Note purpose
Remember why you started writing notes. Maybe it was to learn programming, or write a paper or thesis. Or to write a story or do [[journaling]].

## Do
- Keep things simple: [[minimal notetaking]]
  Don't focus on the meta (note taking)
  Stay focused on the content of your notes, and your goal.
- Just start writing to avoid [[writer's block]]
  Don't worry about structure.
