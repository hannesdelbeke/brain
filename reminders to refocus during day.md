Set a few reminders/[[notification|notifications]] through the day. When the reminder goes off, look back and see if you got distracted.
When you are carried away, you don't notice it. The act of looking back makes you self-aware that you were carried away., and enables you to act on it.

This is mentioned in the book [[Hyperfocus - book|Hyperfocus]].
