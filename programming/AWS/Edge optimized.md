## Edge optimized
**Edge optimized** – A resource that uses [AWS global infrastructure](https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/global-infrastructure/) to better serve geographically diverse clients.

Edge-optimized endpoints are best for geographically distributed clients. This makes them a good choice for public services being accessed from the internet. Regional endpoints are typically used for [[API|APIs]] that are accessed primarily from within the same [[AWS Region]].
