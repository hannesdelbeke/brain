### Similarities
both [[pilates]] and [[yoga]] are 
- full body workouts
- posture improving
- Low impact, slow tempo
### Differences

| Aspect       | Pilates                                 | Yoga                  |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| focus        | Core strength                           | Flexibility & balance |
| Spirituality | Utilitarian                             | Spiritual             |
| Breathing    | Focus on maximizing exercise efficiency | Focus on relaxation   |
