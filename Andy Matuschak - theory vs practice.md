You can read a book about starting a startup, or you can join [Y Combinator](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z9MxCsvv8DcHndqRECbxrRi). Which do you choose? This is the power of an [Enabling environment](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z492hGrHvRvJiEY9UfB4Mby).
- The latter’s a much more effective growth environment, and it’s mostly not the funding.
- **When you read the book, you’re a founder wanna-be reading about the activities of founders, and you’re not “practicing founding”; when you do YC, you’re _doing founding,_ in a community of founders.** The book’s abstract, but YC’s not
