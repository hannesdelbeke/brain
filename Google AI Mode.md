Google AI Mode is like [[Google Search]], but with [[Artificial intelligence|AI]]. 
It focuses on quick results and answers, linking to sources (search results).

[[Gemini]] focusses more on creating content, and is less connected to live news.

**Google AI Mode**: a search tool
