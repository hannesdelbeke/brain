- [[Negotiate like your life depends on it]]
	- you can be empathetic without agreeing. e.g. It seems you were wronged.
	- mirror people by repeating their last 3 words to create rapport.
	- paraphrase in own words
	- people like to be understood, repeating does this better than agreeing.
	- the word fair is very loaded, people use it in negotiating.
	- don't use the word but, it undoes your entire previous sentence.
	- don't use a shit sandwich, start with the bad, then the good.
	- don't skip the small talk, actively listen to their boring rant often is faster to move the conversation on and not have people repeat themselves because they felt you didn't understood them.

### Summary
I mostly remember that Humans are emotional, and want to be understood. 
Also that the secret to happiness is other people, since we are social, and staying busy doing things.

If you make people feel good, you get better at negotiating, and making friends. 
TLDR: focus on pleasing people around you, and you will be happier, and more successful.

the example from the book ... sticked with me the most: A person with anxiety and job issues was happier late in life, than a successful lawyer with hot wife, because they shared their struggles with others in church. And the lawyer kept it to himself, and didn't even share with his wife.

I read a lot of business self help books, the ones related to emotions and conversation seem the most relevant, sticking with me the longest.
